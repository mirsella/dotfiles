Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Verify assumptions instead of guessing. If an assumption can be checked against the codebase, documentation, configuration, logs, tests, or another available source of truth, inspect that source before asking me. If an assumption cannot be verified directly, state the uncertainty clearly and ask for the missing information rather than presenting a guess as fact.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
